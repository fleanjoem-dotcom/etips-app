Earthquake Preparedness Guide

BEFORE Earthquake

🛡️ Prepare & Plan

📋 Preparation Checklist:

  • ✔ Secure Your Space: Anchor heavy furniture and appliances to walls
  • ✔ Emergency Kit: Prepare 72-hour supplies (water, food, first aid)
  • ✔ Identify Safe Spots: Locate sturdy tables in each room
  • ✔ Family Plan: Establish meeting points and communication
  • ✔ Practice Drills: Monthly DROP, COVER, HOLD ON practice
  • ✔ Documents: Keep vital papers in waterproof container

💡 Pro Tip: Review your plan every 6 months and check kit expiration dates.

DURING Earthquake

⚠️ Drop, Cover, Hold On

THE GOLDEN RULE
1️⃣ DROP

Drop to hands and knees immediately

2️⃣ COVER

Cover head and neck under sturdy table

3️⃣ HOLD ON

Hold shelter until shaking stops

📍 Location Actions:

  • 🏠 Indoors: Stay inside, away from windows
  • 🌳 Outdoors: Move to open area, away from buildings
  • 🚗 Vehicle: Pull over safely, stay inside
  • 🏢 High-Rise: No elevators, stay away from windows

⚠️ Warning: Most injuries from falling objects. Protect your head and neck!

AFTER Earthquake

✅ Recover & Stay Safe

🏥 Recovery Steps:

  • ✔ Check Injuries: Provide first aid, get medical help
  • ✔ Inspect Home: Check for structural damage carefully
  • ✔ Gas & Utilities: Turn off gas if you smell leaks
  • ✔ Stay Informed: Listen to radio or E-TIPS for updates
  • ✔ Expect Aftershocks: Be ready to DROP, COVER, HOLD ON
  • ✔ Help Others: Check on neighbors who need assistance
🚫 DO NOT:
  • Use matches or flames until sure no gas leaks
  • Turn on lights until checked for damage
  • Enter damaged buildings
  • Drive unless absolutely necessary

💡 Tip: Emotional recovery is important. Talk about your experience and seek help if needed.
